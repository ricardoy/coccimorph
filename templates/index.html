COCCIMORPH

Remote Diagnosis System

Welcome to COCCIMORPH. This front end allows you to discriminate Eimeria species using oocyst images. In the current version, the system can only discriminate Eimeria species of domestic fowl. We intend to extend its applicability to other parasites in the next future.

COCCIMORPH is a collaborative research work developed by the Laboratory of Molecular Biology of Coccidia at the Departament of Parasitology of the Institute of Biomedical Sciences in São Paulo, and the Cybernetic Vision Research Group, at the Institute of Physics in São Carlos, University of São Paulo.
